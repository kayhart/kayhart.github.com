Veterans Day


As another year passes, we are suddenly upon another Veterans Day. Just as Winston Churchill said, this country owes essentially everything we have to those with the capacity of unconditional love and allegiance to their nation. Our veterans have pledged to defend the core values of this nation for a society of individuals they have never met and without the individual recognition they profoundly deserve.

How We Can Help


So, we must ask ourselves, “What can I do to give back to those who have given so much?” As recruitment professionals, we can give back opportunities. We can give opportunities for our veterans to translate their character and capability into workplace skills. These individuals deserve a chance in our workforce and are in need of employers to believe in them as they have believed in the worthiness of all Americans.

Our soldiers and veterans are incomprehensible individuals with the mental and physical capacity unparalleled by most. So, when we think of our veterans, let it not be that one day a year. Let it not be because we are told. Let us think of our veterans every time we think of love, compassion, and dedication. Let us give back to those in need of employment and provide them a means to a livelihood.
