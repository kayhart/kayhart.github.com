Step 1

Text your word and message to 61222fresh from any cell phone. Like a dictionary FreshText stores the word you add and the "definition" Go ahead and try it!


Step 2

Very shortly after you send your word and message, you'll get a text back letting you know that the storing of your word was successful and it will display your pairing.


Step 3

Once your word as been added it's time to put it to use! Tell your friends, family, co-workers, or your pet chimp BoBo to text your special key word to 61222frestext and they will receive your message!
