Green Products

Our laminate floors don't 'hold' allergens, such as pet dander and dust like carpeting and provides a healthy living environment when cleaned regularly. Laminate flooring is also resistant to the growth of mold and bacteria, especially when treated with special allergen-resistant and anti-bacterial coatings to make them even safer.

Certificates

The high-density core found in our laminate floors is constructed from discarded wood that would otherwise be sent to landfills. Additionally the decorative layer is simply an image of wood, which spares the need for logging of potentially rare species of trees to achieve your desired design.
